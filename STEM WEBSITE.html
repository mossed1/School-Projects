STEM

What is STEM?

STEM is an umbrella term for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics.

STEM Qualities/Skills

  • Critical thinking
  • Problem-solving
  • Creativity
  • Collaboration
  • Communication

STEM Jobs/Real-World Context

Examples of STEM careers include:

  • Engineer
  • Data Scientist
  • Software Developer

STEM is important in various industries such as healthcare, technology, and environmental science.

Statistics

Current statistics on STEM job growth indicate a significant increase in demand for STEM professionals.

Data shows that STEM education is crucial for the workforce, with many high-paying jobs requiring STEM skills.
